# Shepherds

**3D Co-op Action-Adventure** · Producer · 2024 · USC Games MFA Thesis

## Game Overview

A 3D two-player co-op action-adventure. Shepherd Zoe and wolf Kin combine abilities to overcome rhythm-based puzzles and physics-based platforming, descending into an active volcano.

### Characters

**Zoe (Shepherd)** — Aspires to become a professional shepherd like her late mother. Believes in others' goodness.

**Kin (Wolf)** — Seeks its origins. Mistrustful and self-reliant.
