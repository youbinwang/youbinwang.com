# The Scholar's Side Quest

**ARPG** · Level & Combat Designer · Unreal Engine 4 · 2022

## Level Design

A collaborative third-person action-adventure project set in Letter Town — a small town under phantasmagoria's control. Led a team of 17 students to develop the quest flow: 17-page narrative script, 1 major scene, and 13 animation cutscenes in UE4.

## Combat Design

Focused on action feedback and hit response, combining ARPG skill combos with enemy behavior patterns.

## Boss Design

Boss encounters incorporate environmental interaction and phase-based combat mechanics.

### Key Deliverables

- 17-page narrative script
- 1 major scene design
- 13 animation cutscenes
- Environment design and construction
- Level prototype design
