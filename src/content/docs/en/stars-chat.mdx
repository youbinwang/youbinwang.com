# Stars Chat

**Narrative Mobile Game** · Lead Gameplay & Narrative Designer · Unity 2D · 2021

## Game Concept

Players assume the role of author Stefan Zweig, chatting with historical figures through a messaging app called "Stars Chat." Inspired by Zweig's *Decisive Moments in History*.

The core mechanic: "experience defining moments firsthand and assist protagonists in solving problems" to gain permission to write their stories.

## Narrative Design

### Character Research

Each historical figure underwent deep historical research. Dialogue styles reflect authentic personalities and period characteristics.

### Dialogue Writing

Historically-informed dialogues balancing accuracy with gameplay engagement.

## UI/UX

Instant messaging interface design simulating modern social apps, letting players interact with historical figures in a familiar way.

### Quest System

Task flow and item systems designed to progress relationships with historical figures.
