# On the Road

**Board Game / System Design** · Undergraduate Thesis · 2022–2023

## Game Overview

A board game completed independently as an undergraduate thesis, exploring choices and experiences during a journey.

## Core Mechanics / Rulebook

System design-oriented gameplay where players face choices during their journey, managing resources and planning strategies.

## Design Philosophy

Inspired by Kerouac's *On the Road*, recreating the uncertainty and adventure of travel in tabletop form. Prioritizes balance and replayability.

- **Timeline**: September 2022 – June 2023
