# The Journey & Drinks of Elliot Fig

**2.5D Narrative RPG** · Lead Gameplay & Narrative Designer · Unity · 2023

## Narrative Design

A narrative-driven RPG deeply integrating drink-mixing mechanics with storytelling. Players craft beverages to advance the story and influence character relationships.

### Core Competencies

- Game macro writing
- Game flow design
- Media asset management
- Paper prototype validation

## Gameplay Systems

### Drink Mixing

Core gameplay centers on beverage creation — different recipe combinations trigger different narrative branches and character reactions.

### Dialogue System

Implemented with Yarn Spinner, supporting complex branching dialogue and conditional triggers.

## Game Flow Design

Focused on player guidance and narrative pacing, refined through paper prototyping and playtesting iteration.

### Development Tools

- Unity 2.5D
- Yarn Spinner (dialogue system)
- Paper prototypes
- Playtesting & iteration
