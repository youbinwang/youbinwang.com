# The Camera

**2D Top-Down Narrative RPG** · Solo Development · Unity · 2022

## Inspiration & Research

The Camera is a 2D top-down narrative RPG exploring storytelling through a camera's perspective.

## Character

Characters convey personality and story background through unique visual styles and behavioral patterns.

## Story

A story about documentation and memory — players experience fragments of different characters' lives through the camera's viewfinder.

## Scenes

Using a 2D top-down perspective, scene design emphasizes atmosphere and narrative pacing.

## Gameplay

Core gameplay revolves around camera interactions — players advance the story through capturing, recording, and other camera-based mechanics.

## Production Process

Completed in approximately 3 months (April–June 2022), with all design, programming, art, and testing done independently.

- **Engine**: Unity
